Student Opportunity & Skill Development Platform

OpportuNest is designed to help UG & PG students become career-ready by connecting them with the right opportunities and skill paths. It analyzes student resumes, identifies skill gaps, recommends relevant opportunities, and builds consistent learning habits through streak tracking and reminders.

Key Features

Student Onboarding

Degree level, year, domain & city selection

Resume upload + manual skills entry

Resume & Skill Intelligence

Auto-skill extraction

Role match score + missing skills

Opportunity Aggregation

Internships, jobs, hackathons, competitions

Filters based on eligibility, deadline, domain

Learning & Skill Hub

Curated free courses from trusted platforms

Streak-Based Learning

Daily learning motivation with streak progress

Smart Notifications

Alerts for new opportunities & deadlines

Personalized Dashboard

Skill progress, saved opportunities & reminders
